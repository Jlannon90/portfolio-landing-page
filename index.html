About Me

I've lived all over the country, was born in California but spent most of my time in Illinois and Florida. I moved to Portland with my family just after graduating from Florida State Univeristy. Within a few weeks I found employment with a company named Revant Optics and worked in customer service and then community management there. I recently quit that job so I could come learn to code at Epicodus and I'm so excited for what the future has in store!
